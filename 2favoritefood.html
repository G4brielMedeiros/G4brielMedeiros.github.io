My favorite food is the Carrot Cake!

Here's a family recipe for it:

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp baking soda
  • 1 cup corn oil
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 carrots
  • 4 eggs

Preparing

  1. Preheat the oven at 300F
  2. Butter a cake pan and lightly dust with flour
  3. Blend carrots, oil, and eggs
  4. Fill a bowl with the dry ingredients
  5. Pour blender mixture in the bowl
  6. Mix VIOLENTLY
  7. Put the angry mixture in the cake pan
  8. Bring to the oven for 40 minutes
  9. Take it out, and serve happily!
